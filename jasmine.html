jasmine flower

Jasmine genus of plants in the Oleaceae family Jasmine is a genus of plants, Jasminum. They are shrubs or vines that grow in moderately warm climates. There are about 200 different species of Jasminum. They are also a favourite in gardens. Tea can be made from the flowers. Some species are used to make special oil, perfumes or incense. Women, especially from Asia sometimes wear jasmine flowers in their hair. Jasmine flowers are white or yellow in colour, although in rare instances they can be slightly reddish. Jasmine flowers are well known for their lovely smell. Jasmine is also a female name.
