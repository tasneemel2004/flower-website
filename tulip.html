tulip flower

Tulip genus of plants Tulip (Tulipa) is a potflower plant. There is many cultivars and species of tulips. Cultivars are used as ornamental plants. It grows in southern Europe, north Africa, and Asia from Anatolia and Iran in the east to northeast of China and Japan, Indo Asia. It is the national flower of Afghanistan, The Tulip is most associated with Holland. Kingdom: Plantae Divisio: Magnoliophyta Class: Liliopsida Order: Liliales Family: Liliaceae Genus: Tulipa
